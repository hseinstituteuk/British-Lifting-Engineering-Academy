About LEEA UK

The Lifting Equipment Engineers Association (LEEA) is the leading representative body for all those involved with lifting equipment throughout its life cycle of manufacture, supply, ownership, use and disposal.

Established in 1944, LEEA has been at the forefront of lifting equipment safety and standards for over 75 years. Our mission is to promote the safe and effective use of lifting equipment through education, training, and certification programs that meet the highest international standards.
